Dice Game

Each player takes a turn rolling a dice. The player can roll the dice as many times as they want and each roll is added to their score. If the dice lands on 1 however, the player immediately ends their turn and loses all of the points scored during the current turn.

The player can keep their points by hitting hold. The hold button keeps the points earned during the current turn and ends the player's turn.

The winner is the first player to earn 100 points!
